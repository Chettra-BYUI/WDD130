Overview

Purpose

Help new couples plan their wedding with ease.

Audience

My website is for newly engaged couples to have a place to plan their weddings, post pictures and registries. The web page will remain permanently as memoirs for the future generation. On this website there are tools to help them navigate invitation process. It will offer them tips to overcome the pitfalls of scheduling and planning parties for large numbers of people. The website has resources for caterers and other suppliers, where to find wedding dresses and tuxedos. Family and friends can see the progress updates from the couple and see what kind of help they might need. They can also post comments and support to the couple. Everyone can post pictures they have taken from the wedding. This website offers all the necessary resources for new couples to start the eternal journey together that begins with planning a wedding.

Content

Home page

will people come to your site?

Yes people come to visit my site to plan their weddings. They will also source information available. They can look for vendors and other wedding planning resources. Friends and relatives of couple can also come visit the site assist the bride and grooms ond their wedding.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

Vistors will ask about how to manage the number of people at a wedding. They will ask about the customary ways to serve certain food or flowers arrangements. They would like to see samples of center pieces. Pictures of wedding dresses and other attires. I think might ask for security to prevent wedding crushers. Many of these questions maybe answered on a blog or a Q/A page.

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

Visitors will look for a calendar to determine dates. They also would like to have a way to register guess contacts. The home page can provide them a link to add guest registrations. They will also look for ideas for games to entertain the wedding party such as DJ or wedding singer. They probably would like to fill out a mock menue, or schedule for the wedding dinner.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

The guests would need to sign up for an account that requires their first name, last name, and email address. They will also fill out a guest list, if they would like to use other tools. However, this information is private and the site will not use them for commercial purposes. When planning the wedding, the site will ask for the wedding date, location, and venue.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

My site will have no or minimal advertisting. I put my customer first. I will offer a lot of starter free services, and customers can have the options upgrade for additional services if they already exhuasted the free services. The site will not use hard to understand terms or complicated sales tactics.

The home page will dispay general information about the wedding planning business. It will have log in information. Navagation bar to get other sub pages. The other pages includes a resource for wedding dress shopping. Also a site how to plan a wedding if you and your partner live in different countries. It will show pictures of people getting married. It will also have a video link. And an area to show the best voted married couple of the month.
